Here at "Bungie" we focus on creating unique games for your entertainment. We have already created many vast titles, the most popular of said titles being "Halo: Combat Evolved"

Halo: Combat Evolved - Halo is set in the 26th century where you play as a Spartan and face off extraterrestial threats on a strange ring world called Halo.

Halo: Reach - Halo: Reach is a prequel to Halo: Combat Evolved where a team of Spartans attempt to face The Covenant and save Reach.

Halo 3: ODST is set in the 26th century on Earth where a team of ODST's attempt to regroup and destroy and ominous Covenant Corvette hovering over the city of New Mombasa.
